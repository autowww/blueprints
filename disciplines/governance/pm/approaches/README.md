# PM approach guides (blueprint)

**Purpose:** Deeper, **product-agnostic** guides for project management approaches than the short summaries in [`PM.md`](../PM.md). Each file explains the approach on its own terms, links to **authoritative external** material, and states how it **maps** to PM process groups and (where applicable) SDLC phases A–F and PDLC phases P1–P6.

**Audience:** Teams adopting [`blueprints/disciplines/governance/pm/`](../README.md); project-specific overrides stay in project-level planning docs.

| Guide | Topics |
|-------|--------|
| [**PMI/PMBOK**](pmi-pmbok.md) | Standards-based PM body of knowledge; 12 principles, 8 performance domains (7th ed); predictive, Agile, and hybrid tailoring; mapping to PM.md process groups and SDLC/PDLC. |
| [**PRINCE2**](prince2.md) | Process-based controlled-environment PM; 7 principles, 7 themes, 7 processes; management by exception; PRINCE2 Agile integration; mapping to PM.md and SDLC/PDLC. |
| [**Six Sigma / Lean Six Sigma**](six-sigma.md) | Data-driven quality and process improvement; DMAIC and DMADV cycles; belt system; statistical tools; positioning as a PM-adjacent discipline; mapping to SDLC and PDLC. |

**External references:** [`REFERENCE-LINKS.md`](REFERENCE-LINKS.md) — curated URLs with executive summaries.

**Bridge to SDLC and PDLC:** [`PM-SDLC-PDLC-BRIDGE.md`](../PM-SDLC-PDLC-BRIDGE.md) — how these approaches relate to delivery methodologies (Scrum, Kanban, XP, phased) in [`blueprints/sdlc/methodologies/`](../../../../sdlc/methodologies/README.md) and product approaches (Dual-Track, Stage-Gate, Lean Startup) in [`blueprints/pdlc/approaches/`](../../../../pdlc/approaches/README.md).
