# Product

**How we understand users, design experiences, and grow** — the disciplines that ensure we build the right thing, make it usable, and help users succeed.

Product disciplines span the entire product lifecycle — from discovering user needs (BA), through designing experiences (UX), to acquiring users (Marketing) and ensuring their ongoing success (Customer Success).

| Discipline | Core question | Package | Deep dives |
|-----------|---------------|---------|------------|
| [**Product Management**](product-management/README.md) | Are we building the right product for the right market, and is our strategy coherent? | Vision, strategy, roadmap, prioritization, market analysis, competitive intelligence, business model, product-market fit, OKRs, discovery cadence | |
| [**Business Analysis**](ba/README.md) | What do stakeholders need, and does the solution satisfy those needs? | BABOK knowledge areas, elicitation techniques, perspectives, requirements lifecycle | [Techniques](ba/techniques/README.md) · [Knowledge areas](ba/knowledge-areas/README.md) · [Perspectives](ba/perspectives/README.md) |
| [**UX / UI Design**](ux-design/README.md) | Is the product usable, desirable, and accessible? | Design thinking, interaction design, visual design, research methods, design systems, accessibility | [Techniques](ux-design/techniques/README.md) |
| [**Marketing**](marketing/README.md) | How do we acquire, engage, and retain users? | Digital marketing, growth engineering, product-led growth, positioning, analytics, GTM execution | [Channels](marketing/channels/README.md) · [Growth](marketing/growth/README.md) |
| [**Customer Success**](customer-success/README.md) | How do we help users achieve their goals and reduce churn? | Onboarding, customer health scoring, support operations, feedback loops, churn prevention | [Practices](customer-success/practices/README.md) |

## Relationship to other families

| Family | How Product relates |
|--------|---------------------|
| [**Engineering**](../engineering/README.md) | Product defines *what* to build; Engineering builds it. UX deliverables (wireframes, design tokens) are implemented by Frontend/Mobile. BA requirements feed SDLC phases A–B. |
| [**Data**](../data/README.md) | Product depends on data for decision-making — analytics, A/B experiments, customer health metrics. Data Science provides the modeling that Marketing and Customer Success use for segmentation and prediction. |
| [**Governance**](../governance/README.md) | PM governs the delivery of product decisions. Product priorities (from BA, Marketing insights) flow into PM's backlog management and sprint planning. |
| [**Security**](../security/README.md) | Security controls (auth flows, consent dialogs, error messaging) have direct UX implications. Marketing must handle user data securely. |
| [**Compliance**](../compliance/README.md) | Product decisions are constrained by compliance — GDPR affects data collection (Marketing), WCAG shapes design (UX), COPPA restricts features for children. |
