# BA templates

Copy-paste starters for business analysis artifacts. Copy into your project's `docs/product/`, `docs/requirements/`, or equivalent folder and fill in.

| Template | Knowledge Area | Purpose |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| [`stakeholder-register.template.md`](stakeholder-register.template.md) | BA Planning & Monitoring | Identify stakeholders, assess influence/interest, plan engagement strategies |
| [`business-case.template.md`](business-case.template.md) | Strategy Analysis | Quantify costs, benefits, risks, and recommendation for an investment decision |
| [`requirements-package.template.md`](requirements-package.template.md) | Requirements Analysis & Design Definition | Structured requirements specification with traceability and acceptance criteria |
| [`decision-analysis.template.md`](decision-analysis.template.md) | Strategy Analysis, RADD | Structured evaluation of alternatives using weighted criteria |
