Extreme Programming (XP)

Technical practices: TDD, CI, small releases, pairing — often combined with Scrum or Kanban cadence.

Process overview

  • XP emphasizes technical excellence: feedback loops through tests, integration, collaboration, and continuous design improvement.
  • It is compatible with Scrum (iteration) or Kanban (flow) — XP answers “how we build,” not the planning calendar by itself.
  • CI/CD & DoD in this blueprint map directly to XP practices; agents increase the need for automated gates and human review.
