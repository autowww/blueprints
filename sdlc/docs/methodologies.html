Methodologies & tracking

Comprehensive guides for 19 methodologies organized by group — iterative Agile, sequential/risk-driven, scaling, Lean/continuous delivery, complementary practices — all mapped to one engineering-tracking foundation.

Project tracking files under the consuming repo’s sdlc/ (e.g. TRACKING-METHODOLOGIES.md) stay lightweight. Full methodology guides (Scrum, Kanban, …) live in blueprints/sdlc/methodologies/ — canonical Markdown + handbook pages below.

Cross-cutting scope management and time tracking (git activity vs explicit hours) are in the Change control chapter — methodology docs below assume you align backlog and work-unit ids with those rules. Spec-driven development (acceptance criteria and durable intent before deep implementation) is a separate handbook chapter — complementary to every methodology here.

Methodology guides (deep dives)

Each topic has a handbook page (summary, diagrams, external links with executive summaries) and Markdown in the blueprint. Scrum, Kanban, phased delivery, and XP each have four subchapters (Foundation, Roles, Ceremonies, Process) with tables, mini-TOC, and Mermaid — regenerated from docs/build_methodology_chapters.py (Scrum Roles is hand-maintained). Canonical depth stays in methodologies/<name>/. For SDD-style inputs/outputs per ceremony intent and cross-methodology process slots, use Ceremonies (SDD I/O) and Process (SDD I/O) under Spec-driven development.
