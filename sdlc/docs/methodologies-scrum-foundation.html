Scrum — Foundation & fit

How Scrum maps to phases A–F, the tracking spine, and C1–C6. Canonical: foundation-connection.md · scrum.md.

Overview

Scrum plugs into the shared tracking spine and ceremony intents C1–C6. It adds timeboxes, the three accountabilities, and Scrum commitments—not a replacement for traceability.

Phases A–F in Scrum

PhaseScrum expressionNotes
A — ShapeBacklog ordering, stakeholder inputPO leads; Developers may join discovery
B — PlanSprint Planning (what)Select PBIs for Sprint Goal
C — BuildSprint execution, Daily ScrumScope changes only with PO if goal at risk
D — VerifyReview + DoDIntegrated increment
E — ReleasePotentially shippable incrementRelease cadence may differ from sprint
F — Operate & learnRetro; ops handoff if applicableImprove process; feed backlog

Rule: Each sprint touches B–E at minimum. A is continuous (refinement). F every sprint.
