Kanban — Foundation & fit

Foundation fit for Kanban teams.

Overview

Kanban optimizes flow with visualization, policies, and WIP limits. Tracking and C1–C6 still apply. Canonical: foundation-connection.md.

Phases A–F

PhaseKanban expression
AOptions / funnel / discovery
BReplenishment → committed columns
CPull per Definition of Workflow
DColumn DoD / QA swimlanes
ERelease policy (on demand, train)
FSDR, retros, upstream feedback

Rule: No replenishment + no review cadence = board only, not a managed system.

Tracking spine

ArtifactKanban mapping
IntentCard at intake; may be off-board
SpecLinked doc / AC on card
PlanPolicies + commitment point
TasksSubtasks / child cards
PRsLinked; policy may require before merge
ReviewsPer transition policy
ReleaseTrain or continuous

Ceremony intents

IntentTypical ceremony
C1Replenishment; prioritization
C2Refinement before pull
C3Stand-up / workflow meeting
C4QA / release checklist
C5Retro; systems thinking
C6Stakeholder review; docs

Archetypes

RoleArchetypes
SRM / PMSponsor proxy, Orchestrator
Delivery teamImplementer, Quality advocate
CoachOrchestrator, Quality advocate

Conceptual commitments

  • Visualization
  • WIP limits / policies
  • Explicit Definition of Workflow
  • Manage flow (lead time, predictability)

Anti-patterns

Anti-patternFix
Infinite columns, no WIPMinimal workflow + limits
Urgent bypass lane abuseSteer authorizes class of service
Dev-only boardExtend upstream visibility
